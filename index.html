About Wedding Wows

From the heart of Jammu, Wedding Wows by Ankita brings your dream wedding to life. Our NIFT Hyderabad alumni founder blends the regal artistry of Dogra heritage with contemporary design to create breathtaking bridal wear.

Our Story of Heritage & Craft

Founded with a vision to make every bride feel like royalty, Wedding Wows is a celebration of Jammu's rich cultural tapestry. We specialize in bespoke bridal couture, elegant pret wear, and exquisite accessories, drawing inspiration from timeless Dogra craftsmanship. Each piece is an heirloom, meticulously handcrafted to perfection.
